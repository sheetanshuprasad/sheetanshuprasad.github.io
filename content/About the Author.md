Hi, I'm **Sheetanshu Prasad**.

I'm a music producer, mixing and mastering engineer, composer, songwriter, and sound designer with a passion for helping musicians create better music and build sustainable careers.

Like many producers, I started with a simple dream—to make great music.

But as I continued learning, I realized that making music was only one part of the journey.

Finding clients, pricing services, marketing music, building a portfolio, and earning a consistent income were challenges that very few people talked about.

That realization eventually led to the creation of **MusicMonetized**.

## My Journey

Over the years, I've spent thousands of hours learning music production, mixing, mastering, recording, and audio engineering.

Along the way, I've worked on various music and film projects, continually improving both my technical skills and my understanding of the music industry.

My experience includes:

* Music production
* Mixing and mastering
* Songwriting and composition
* Sound design
* Home studio recording
* Audio editing and restoration

I've also had the opportunity to contribute to award-winning short film projects, experiences that taught me the importance of collaboration, storytelling, and attention to detail.

## Why I Started MusicMonetized

When I searched online for guidance, I found thousands of tutorials explaining:

* EQ
* Compression
* Reverb
* Plugins
* Music theory

But very few people explained questions like:

* How do music producers actually earn money?
* How do you get your first client?
* How should you price your services?
* How do you market your music?
* How do you build a long-term career?

I created MusicMonetized to help answer those questions.

The goal isn't simply to teach music production.

The goal is to help producers build careers they can sustain for years.

## What You Can Expect

Every article published on MusicMonetized is written with practical value in mind.

Rather than chasing trends or shortcuts, I focus on sharing information that can genuinely help producers improve their skills and make smarter career decisions.

Topics you'll regularly find include:

* Music production
* Mixing and mastering
* Home studios
* Freelancing
* Client acquisition
* Music marketing
* Beat selling
* Career growth
* Music business

My aim is to make complex topics easier to understand while keeping the advice practical and actionable.

## My Philosophy

I believe success in music comes from combining creativity with consistency.

Technical skills matter.

Business skills matter.

Relationships matter.

Continuous learning matters.

There are no shortcuts to building a lasting career, but with patience, curiosity, and consistent effort, it's absolutely possible.
