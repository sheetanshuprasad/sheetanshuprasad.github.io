About the Author

My name is Sheetanshu.

I created MusicMonetized to share insights about music production, mixing, mastering, freelancing, and building a sustainable career in the audio industry.

My goal is to help producers, mix engineers, and musicians improve both their technical skills and their ability to earn income from music.

This website documents lessons, strategies, and experiences related to audio production and music business.
